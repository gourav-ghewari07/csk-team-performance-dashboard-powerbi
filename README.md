🦁 CSK Team Performance Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The CSK Team Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze the historical performance of Chennai Super Kings (CSK) players and team statistics.

The dashboard provides insights into batting performance, bowling effectiveness, fielding contributions, and player achievements, helping cricket enthusiasts understand the team's success through data-driven analysis.

---

🎯 Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

- Analyze overall CSK team performance.
- Evaluate batting and bowling statistics.
- Compare player contributions.
- Visualize fielding records and achievements.
- Generate insights from IPL performance data.

🔍 Key Insights

1. Strong Team Performance

CSK has maintained strong batting and bowling statistics across a large number of matches, reflecting long-term consistency.

2. High Batting Strike Rate

The strike rate of 128.60 indicates an aggressive and effective batting approach.

3. Significant Bowling Contributions

A bowling average of 17.17 demonstrates strong wicket-taking ability and bowling efficiency.

4. Fielding Plays a Major Role

The team recorded over 341 catches and 31 stumpings, highlighting the importance of fielding in match outcomes.

5. Boundary Scoring Drives Success

A large proportion of runs come through boundaries, emphasizing aggressive batting strategies.

6. Key Players Influence Team Results

Several experienced players consistently contribute through batting, bowling, and fielding performances.
